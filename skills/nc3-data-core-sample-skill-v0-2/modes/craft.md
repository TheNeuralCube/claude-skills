# Craft lens: engineering craft study

## Deliverable

`{date}_{slug}_craft-study_core-sample.md`, frontmatter and conventions per [../references/deliverable-contract.md](../references/deliverable-contract.md), lens `craft`.

## Consumer

Execution-class, cold; the primary human reader is the operator, learning from the artifact's author.

## Required sections

1. TL;DR naming the author's engineering center of gravity in 3 sentences.
2. Inferred worldview principles: 5 to 10, each stated in one line then evidenced with citations.
3. Build-order archaeology: growth rings inferred from dependency direction and comment vintage, framed as methodology (what the author built first and why that order teaches something).
4. Language/framework craft teaching sections, calibrated to the operator's stated level. Explain idioms, cite real files. Use the operator's preferred analogy domains where they genuinely map: Cisco UC, oil and gas systems, networking. Do not force an analogy that does not map.
5. Comment and documentation philosophy: a taxonomy table (species of comment/doc, example citation, extractable rule).
6. Naming and structure conventions.
7. Process and ops style signals (tooling, scripts, release habits, guardrails).
8. Collaboration predictions: "what this predicts the author will expect", ranked, explicitly labeled as informed extrapolation.
9. A sequenced learning path with exercises that produce showable artifacts.
10. A bridge glossary anchored to the operator's existing expertise.
11. Closing calibration: honest gap assessment between the operator and the author, guarding against both false parity and self-erasure, per the operator's Inverse Confidence Rule in both directions.

## Evidence emphasis

Comments, tests, tooling files, docs hierarchy, commit conventions if visible. Style evidence outranks feature evidence: how the author writes matters more here than what the code does. Shared evidence base per [../references/evidence-protocol.md](../references/evidence-protocol.md).

## War-game applicability

Applies to the collaboration predictions: steelman each top prediction per [../references/war-game-protocol.md](../references/war-game-protocol.md) step 1, plus the assumptions ledger, consumer simulation, and fluff purge. Predictions that lose their steelman are demoted or cut.

## Quality bar

The operator finishes the document knowing what to DO differently next week, not just what the author is like. Every principle and idiom claim cites a real file. The learning path is executable without further research.
